How to Order?

Order can be made via WhatsApp. Please refer to the steps below:

  1. Choose the flavour you want.
  2. Select the quantity of the product.
  3. Contact our staff to place order 📱.
  4. Select the delivery option.
  5. Select your payment method.
  6. Check your mailbox 📧 to receive order confirmation details.
